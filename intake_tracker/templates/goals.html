Strength Training

Goal: Increase overall strength and power.

Approach: Focus on compound movements (squats, deadlifts, bench presses) with low-to-moderate reps and heavy weights.

Key Elements: High weight, low reps, long rest periods, and progressive overload.

Hypertrophy Training

Goal: Maximize muscle size and growth.

Approach: Higher volume training with moderate weights and higher rep ranges, focusing on muscle fatigue and pump.

Key Elements: Moderate weights, high reps (8-12), and short rest periods.

HIIT

Goal: Improve cardiovascular fitness and burn fat.

Approach:Short bursts of intense activity followed by brief rest periods, often combining bodyweight and cardio exercises.
